## Doko Demo Issyo PSP Fan Translation
A work-in-progress English translation for the Japanese life simulator/communication game *Doko Demo Issyo (どこでもいっしょ)*, released on the PlayStation Portable in 2004.

[https://github.com/pumpkinhasapatch/dokodemo-psp-english](https://github.com/pumpkinhasapatch/dokodemo-psp-english)
